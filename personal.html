Bullet Journal

Bullet journaling, for me, is a way of relaxing my mind. I started keeping a bullet journal in 2017 and found that it helped me in becoming more organized, remembering events, and getting ready for the week ahead. I particuarly enjoy creating each week's layout and brush calligraphy writing. Even if I wind up not adding to it daily, it still helps me be more efficient in my day to day tasks.
